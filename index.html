How to play

Touch or click the green start marker (outside the maze) to begin. Move into the maze, navigate through the interior, then return to the red exit outside the maze. Avoid touching black walls.

Tip: The blue player follows your pointer smoothly. A trail of dots shows your path. Timer and score update while you move.
